Collaborations and Relationships


Ariana Grande
Mac Miller and Ariana Grande collaborated on several occasions, including the hit song "The Way," which was released in 2013. The two were also in a relationship for nearly two years before breaking up in 2018.

Anderson .Paak
Mac Miller and Anderson .Paak collaborated on the song "Dang!" in 2016, which was featured on Mac's album "The Divine Feminine."

Pharrell Williams
Mac Miller worked with Pharrell Williams on his 2015 album "GO:OD AM," specifically on the songs "Weekend" and "In the Bag."

Ab-Soul
Mac Miller and Ab-Soul collaborated on the song "The End Is Near" in 2012, which was featured on the "Macadelic" mixtape. Schoolboy Q - Mac Miller and Schoolboy Q collaborated on the song "Gees" in 2013, which was featured on Mac's album "Watching Movies with the Sound Off."

Earl Sweatshirt
Mac Miller and Earl Sweatshirt collaborated on the song "Guild" in 2013, which was featured on Mac's album "Watching Movies with the Sound Off."
