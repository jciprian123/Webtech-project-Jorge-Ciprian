About Me

My name is Jorge Ciprian and was born and raised in New Jersey. My mother is from Colombia & my father is from Cuba. Growing up I always loved to play soccer. Since I was a goalie my favorite player was always Iker Casillas.

I chose to come to Babson College because ever since I was little I wanted to become an entreprenuer. I wanted to have my own business creating all of the crazy ideas that came to my mind. When looking at colleges I visited Babson and saw their focus on entreprenuership & knew it was the place for me.

My Interests

My favorite hobby is thrifting clothing. I like to find old used and vintage clothing and give them new life. I also like to take the clothing and turn it into new things. Creating clothing is another one of my hobbies and a lot of times the clothing from the thrift is used for materials to make new clothing. I often sell the items that I purchase on a website called Grailed.
